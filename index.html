Here are my top 10 favorite restaurants, along with some basic information about each one:

  1. Applebee's Grill + Bar

    Applebee's isnt the best restaurant, however the selection and how common they are make it number 1 on my list

  2. Chili's Grill & Bar

    Similar to number 1 slot Chili's has a great food selection

  3. Buffalo Wild Wings

    serves great food & drinks.

  4. Red Robin

    Pretty decent burgers with a large brew selection

  5. Cheesecake Factory

    The normal food is pretty decent however the main reason for going here is the cheesecake

  6. Texas Roadhouse

    The rolls are some of the best in the game

  7. Outback Steakhouse

    great steaks

  8. Red Lobster

    excellent seafood however, it really depends on the location

  9. Olive Garden

    My Italian friends say its an embarrassment to Italy however, i always enjoy the food and drinks

  10. Cracker Barrel

    Ive only had breakfast here but its my sons favorite.
